The TCMS motto, “Putting People Over Paperwork,” sums up the mission of this system. To successfully run a needs clearinghouse, such as those run by Love INC affiliates, there is a lot of paperwork involved. Clients must be tracked, along with their needs; these needs must be verified; resources for goods and services must be known; volunteers must be known and sent communications to keep them involved. All of this adds up to a significant recordkeeping (i.e., paperwork) burden.

TCMS addressed these issues by providing a repository for all clearinghouse operation needs. It helped eliminate duplication of work, and also linke clients with goods and services. It also provided the means to communicate with volunteers, either en masse or using selective criteria. This allowed the recordkeeping requirement to be smaller and quicker, giving more time for the actual helping of clients. It used WordPress for its front end; this provided organizations with a web presence that they could maintain without having to employ a web guru, using WordPress’s WYSIWYG editor and management tools.

TCMS was decommissioned in 2014.
